# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: May 3, 2024**

This Comflowy Privacy Policy (“Privacy Policy”) describes how your personal information is processed when you use Comflowy, a product that uses AI models like Stable Diffusion to realize functions such as text-to-image and image-to-image generation.

## Information We Collect, How It Is Used, & The Legal Basis for Processing

**Account Information.** We collect your email address or phone number to create an account and verify your identity. We also create a user ID for your Comflowy account. We may use this information to communicate with you about your account. This is so we can comply with our contractual obligations to you.

**Device Information.** We collect your device type and operating system details for analytics and to troubleshoot product issues. This is so we can comply with our contractual obligations to you.

**Communication Records.** We maintain a record when you communicate with us via email, through the Comflowy, or otherwise. We store these communications and information related to these messages, such as the date of the communication and to whom it was sent.

**Third Party Application Login.** You can log into your Comflowy account using third party applications (e.g., Google , Microsoft and Github). When you log in using this method, we collect your name and email address associated with that third party application for authentication and identity verification purposes. This processing is necessary to comply with our contractual obligations to you.

**Subscription Billing Data.** We use a third party service provider to collect and process your billing information when you subscribe to Comflowy. The information our billing provider collects may include your name, billing address, your credit or debit card details, phone number, and email address. This processing is necessary to comply with our contractual obligations to you.

**Advertising.** We collect information about you when you interact with Comflowy ads to measure the effectiveness of our ad campaigns. This information includes your IP address, advertising ID, and ad interaction information. It is in our legitimate business interest to advertise our product. Where necessary, we will obtain your consent before engaging in this process.

**User Research.** We may ask you to help us test new features or participate in surveys to help enhance your Comflowy experience. Your participation is voluntary and the data we collect is anonymized. With your consent, we may use your personal information to communicate with you.

**Analytics.** To gather analytics and metrics to better understand how users use the Comflowy. We also use analytics data to evaluate and improve the Comflowy, including the Ad Services and personalization, and to develop new products and services. It is in our legitimate business interests to make informed decisions about product development.

## How We Share Your Information

**Service Providers.** We share your information with third-party service providers who use this information to perform services for us, such as payment processors, hosting providers, auditors, advisors, consultants, customer service providers, as well as service providers who assist us in providing the Ad Services.

**Business Transfers.** We may disclose or transfer your information as part of any merger, sale, and transfer of our assets, acquisition or restructuring of all or part of our business, bankruptcy, or similar event.

**Affiliates.** We may share Your information with Our affiliates, in which case we will require those affiliates to honor this Privacy Policy. Affiliates include Our parent company and any other subsidiaries, joint venture partners or other companies that We control or that are under common control with Us.

**With your consent.** At any time during your use of the Services, or upon explicit request from us, you may consent to the disclosure of your information.

**Interactions with Comflowy.** Our third party LLM providers and third party bot developers may receive details about your interactions with Comflowy (including the contents of your chats, image, etc.) to provide and generally improve their services, which they may process in their legitimate business interests. Your Comflowy user account information (e.g., username and email) is not shared with any third party LLMs or developers. Third party developers that create bots on Comflowy using APIs may view and store your chats on their servers in order to train their models.
Keep in mind, any information you provide to the bots on Comflowy will be shared with third party LLM providers and developers powering the bots, and there is no need to share sensitive personal information with the bots (e.g., credit card information, social security information, etc.). 

**For legal or regulatory purposes.** We also reserve the right to disclose your information without your consent to comply with any applicable law, regulation, legal process or governmental requests.

## How We Secure and Store Your Information

**Security.** Comflowy maintains appropriate technical, administrative, and physical safeguards to protect the information we collect from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, destruction, and altercation.

**Retention.** We keep your personal information for as long as we have a relationship with you. Once our relationship with you has come to an end, we will retain your personal information for a period of time that enables us to maintain business records for audit purposes, comply with record retention requirements under the law, and defend or bring any existing or potential legal claims.

## Your Privacy Rights

**Access your Information.** You may be entitled under data protection laws to access and review Personal Information the Company holds related to you. You may access, modify or delete the information we collected by editing your profile or controlling the content that you share at any time.

**Data retention.** We retain your Information for as long as necessary to deliver the Services, to comply with any applicable legal requirements, to maintain security and prevent incidents and, in general, to pursue our legitimate interests.

## Children's Privacy
We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from anyone under the age of 13 or knowingly allow such persons to create a Comflowy account. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 we take steps to delete that account.

## Changes to this Privacy Policy

We will let You know via email and/or a prominent notice on Our Service, prior to the change becoming effective and update the "Last updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy.
You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
