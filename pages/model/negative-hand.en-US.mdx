### Comflowy Review

The reasons for recommending this embedding are twofold:

1. It has specific optimizations for hands in figures.
2. It won't cause a change in the image style due to the addition of Negative Prompt Embedding. Some Negative Embeddings, although helpful in improving image quality, can change the style of the generated images. However, this Negative Hand will not.

### Best Practices
Simply enter embedding:negative_hands in the Negative Prompt to use it.

If you want to adjust the weight, add it after the embedding, for example, embedding:negative_hands:0.5.
