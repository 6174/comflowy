* [**BeamO: The World's First Four-in-One Life Monitor**](https://www.withings.com/be/en/beam-o)

BeamO integrates a digital stethoscope, medical-grade single-lead electrocardiogram (ECG), pulse oximeter, and non-contact thermometer. It can observe the heart and lungs, detect atrial fibrillation (AFib), provide notifications for high or low heart rate, detect elevated body temperature, and display potential signs of decreasing blood oxygen saturation. The collected data records can be interpreted by a doctor or AI. A complete health check can be completed in just one minute.

* [**Resemble Enhance: Open Source Speech Super-Resolution AI Model**]( https://huggingface.co/spaces/ResembleAI/resemble-enhance)

It can convert noisy audio into clear and powerful speech, removing various noises and disturbances from the audio while preserving the clear speech. Not only can it eliminate noise, but it can also restore audio distortion and extend audio bandwidth. It makes the original sound clearer and more natural. For example, it can turn old cassette recordings into high-definition audio, making them sound more comfortable to listen to. When it comes to the restoration and recovery of historical recordings or archived audio, Resemble Enhance provides an effective solution, breathing new life into these audio recordings.
